Replay

by Nicola Wren

Fringe

3–4 December 2017

New Theatre Studio A

“I know how hard it is to forgive someone you love more than anything for leaving” ​… When a fiercely independent, workaholic police officer finds herself on the street where her brother once lived, she is propelled back to her vibrant childhood and forced to confront a tremendous loss. Intimate, moving and ultimately uplifting, ‘Replay’ by Nicola Wren is the story of a woman revisiting her childhood.

Combining acting and shadow puppetry, this new monologue explores through heart, honesty and humour what it means to grow up, accept loss, be vulnerable and celebrate the past, however painful.

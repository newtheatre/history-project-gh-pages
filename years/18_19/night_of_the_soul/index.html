Night of the Soul

by David Farr

Postgrads

16–19 January 2019

New Theatre

Joanna is stuck cleaning hotel rooms. She spends days and nights there, but nobody seems to notice her. Francis is stuck with his inability to forgive himself. From hotel to hotel, he seeks asylum from his self-inflicted sentence away from his family. They both need to break up with the past and move on. They need help…

Here, the World Entire

by Anwen Kya Hayward
Adapted by Sam Osborne

Fringe

11–12 March 2019

Djanogly Terrace

Medusa lives alone on the outskirts of the world, having exiled herself for her own protection. Sheâ€™s brought enough hurt to the world already. Perseus arrives and, despite her attempts to make him leave, stays. He persists, and as the days wear on she reveals more about the events that led her to the cave. Medusa must make a choice: stay safe and alone, or re-enter the world with Perseus. One question still remains, however: what does Perseus want?
